About me

Currently, I am working for OPPO as a senior researcher in Video Coding Standard Team (Nebula Lab. in OPPO Research Institute). My research interests include image/video compression, neural network based video coding(hybrid framework), E2E based video coding, super resolution and image processing.

  • Familiar with H.265/HEVC, H.266/VVC and AVS3 standards.

  • Familiar with HM, JEM, VTM, HPM, ECM and NNVC reference software.

  • Familiar with the video coding standardization process.
